documentary

The short documentary was made in a course called Creating New Media for the Old & New West with visiting instructor Don Grusin at the University of Colorado. It was partially sponsored by the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) to get youth involved with the BLM besides typical recreational purposes. Also sponsored by the Center for American West. We learned the history of the west and BLM in the class then worked on this film for the duration of the semester.

play

This stop-motion film was made in a course called Digital Media 1 as a part of the Technology, Arts & Media Minor through ATLAS Institute. My main goal for this project was to tell a story and create characters that show emotion.

work

As an employee for the CU Environmental Center, I made this video about the Green Stampede. Green Stampede is an effort to make all football games zero waste events and involves educating spectators and vendors in the stadium.
